Oatmeal Pecan Waffles Recipe

This waffle recipe is a delicious twist on traditional waffles, incorporating the wholesome goodness of oats and the rich flavor of pecans.

Ingredients

  • l cup of whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup oats (quick -cooking)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup nuts (pecans, unsalted, chopped)
  • 2 eggs (large, separated)
  • 1 1/2 cups milk (fat-free (skim))
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cups berries (strawberries, halved)
  • 1 cup berries (blackberries)
  • 1 cup berries (blueberries)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (powdered)

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Whisk wet ingredients together in a separate bowl
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle to medium heat
  5. Pour batter onto skillet, cook until bubbles form on surface
  6. Flip pancakes and cook until golden brown on both sides
  7. Serve warm with syrup and butter
