Buttermilk Pancakes

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • flour
  • buttermilk
  • sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Whisk wet ingredients together in a separate bowl
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle to medium heat
  5. Pour batter onto skillet, cook until bubbles form on surface
  6. Flip pancakes and cook until golden brown on both sides
  7. Serve warm with syrup and butter
